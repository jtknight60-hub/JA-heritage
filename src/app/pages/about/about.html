About JA Heritage

JA Heritage was founded to bring the authentic flavors and crafts of Jamaica to a wider audience. We work directly with small producers — farmers, roasters, and artisans — to source high-quality products that reflect Jamaica's rich culinary and cultural traditions.

Our mission is to create sustainable, fair partnerships that benefit local communities while offering customers transparent, traceable goods. From Blue Mountain coffee to handwoven baskets, every product is selected for quality and provenance.

We value sustainability, fair pay, and strong relationships with our producers. If you'd like to learn more about our sourcing or to partner with us, please reach out via the Contact page.
